Our Process

Our structured workflow encompasses five critical phases: Initial Consultation & Concept, Feasibility Analysis & Architectural Design, Compliance Approvals & Permitting, Active Construction & Execution, and finally, Handover & Lifecycle Support.
Timelines vary based on scope and scale. A typical residential build may span 8 to 12 months, whereas comprehensive commercial complexes can take between 1.5 to 3 years. A detailed milestone schedule is provided during the detailed planning phase.

Pricing & Payment

Costs are meticulously formulated considering the total square footage, targeted material quality standards, architectural complexity, and prevailing market rates for labor. We present a comprehensive, granular Bill of Quantities (BOQ) before securing any commitment.
No. We maintain strict financial integrity. Every known cost is documented in the final agreement. In the event of unforeseen requirements or client-requested changes, transparent cost adjustments are discussed, formalized, and approved strictly prior to execution.
We accommodate all standard institutional methods including direct bank transfers (NEFT/RTGS), certified cheques, and applicable corporate payment protocols suitable for varying scales of commercial and residential invoicing.
Yes. Our payment structure is strictly tied to project milestones. Funding is released progressively as specific predefined construction phases (e.g., site excavation, slab casting, MEP installations) pass inspection and are successfully concluded.
